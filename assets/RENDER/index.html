About AgentCanary

AgentCanary is cross-asset macro market intelligence built for AI agents and traders. We publish four time-stamped briefs every day — Macro Radar at 03:15 UTC (overnight setup), Signal Scan at 09:15 (cross-asset prep), Market Pulse at 15:15 (intraday crypto microstructure), and Market Wrap at 21:15 (close synthesis) — covering regime classification, risk gauge, forward scenarios, top movers, sector strength, whale flows, derivatives positioning, and intel themes. Every brief is permanently archived as a timestamped page; predictions are scored against actual market outcomes 48-72 hours later. Access via REST API, MCP server (npx agentcanary-mcp), JSON Feed, RSS, or the ClawHub skill. AI crawlers welcomed via llms.txt and an explicit robots.txt allowlist.
